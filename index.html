Idli Sambhar

Idli Sambar is a hearty, satisfying, comforting and a healthy meal of soft fluffy idlis served with savory, spiced and lightly tangy sambar – a vegetable stew made with lentils and assorted vegetables. It is a winning combination made for each other.

Ingredients For Iddli

  • Idli rice – 2 cups
  • Urd dal – 1/2 cup
  • Poha – 1/4 cup
  • Salt to taste

Ingredients For Sambhar

  • Arhar ki dal – 1/2 cup
  • Turmeric powder – 1/2 tsp
  • Oil – 2 tsp
  • Mustard seeds – 1/2 tsp
  • Curry leaves – 10-12
  • Tomato – 1 cup (chopped)
  • Sambhar masala – 2 tbsp
  • Brinjal – 1 (chopped)
  • Tamarind pulp – 2 tbsp

Instructions For Idli

  1. Step 1: Wash and soak rice poha and urd dal separately for 4-5 hours.
  2. Step 2: Drain and blend in a blender together with salt and poha till smooth.
  3. Step 3: Transfer the batter in a big vessel and cover and keep to ferment for 6-8 hou
  4. Step 4: Grease the idli molds and pour the batter in each mold.
  5. Step 5: Steam the idlies for 15-20 minutes, till done.

Instructions For Sambhar

  1. Step 1: Wash the dal.
  2. Step 2: Add it in a pressure cooker with salt and turmeric powder.
  3. Step 3: Cook till done.
  4. Step 4: Add oil in a pan.
  5. Step 5: When the oil is hot, add mustard seeds and curry leaves.
  6. Step 6: Add onion and fry for 2 minutes
  7. Step 7: Add tomatoes, brinjal, and sambhar masala.
  8. Step 8: Add half a cup of water and cook till brinjal is soft.2

  10. Step 10: Cook for another 4-5 minutes.
  11. Step 11: Serve hot with idlies.

Instructions For Coconut Chutney

  1. Step 1: Grind coconut, putnaala pappu and green chillis adding about 1/2 cup of water to a fine paste
  2. Step 2: Heat 1 tsp oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter. Add the urad dal and slightly brown it. Finally add curry leaves, remove from fire and add to the ground coconut paste.
  3. Step 3: Serve with hot idlis.
